Subnautica Guide
Step by step guide to setup mods on latest the version of Subnautica
  BepInEx Pack   Mod Manager   SMLHelper
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Download the three mods above

Place the zip files into your game folder (click me)

Extract  BepInEx_x64_5.4.21.0.zip  into your game folder

Launch the game to generate a  plugins  folder within  BepInEx\

Move the  SMLHelper 2.15.0.1  and  Subnautica Mod Manager  zip files
to the  BepInEx\plugins\  folder, then extract/unzip both

Delete left over zip files, make sure you delete the empty \plugins\plugins\
folder after you drag  Modding Helper  folder out of it

You're good to go now! Happy modding, and for any further
questions, you can just ask in the Subnautica Modding discord
